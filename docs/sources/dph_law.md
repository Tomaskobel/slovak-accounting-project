# DPH Law (Act 222/2004 Z.z.) - Structured Extract

## Law Structure Overview

The law is organized into 8 main parts (Casti):

| Part | Title | Sections | Engine relevance |
|------|-------|----------|-----------------|

| 3 | Tax Liability (Tax Point) | ss 19-21 | When tax becomes due |
| 4 | Tax Base and Rates | ss 22-27 | Amount calculation, rate selection |

## PART 1: GENERAL PROVISIONS (ss 1-7a)

### s 1 - Predmet upravy (Subject Matter)

**Topics:** scope, territorial application
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:
- Regulates VAT on supply of goods and services in Slovakia
- Applies to acquisition of goods from other EU member states
- Applies to import of goods into Slovakia

### s 2 - Predmet dane (Subject of Tax)

**Topics:** taxable transactions, four categories
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:
- Four categories of taxable transactions:
  1. Supply of goods for consideration in Slovakia by a taxable person
  2. Supply of services for consideration in Slovakia by a taxable person
  3. Acquisition of goods from another EU member state for consideration in Slovakia
  4. Import of goods into Slovakia

### s 3 - Zdanitelna osoba (Taxable Person)

**Topics:** taxable person definition
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:
- Any person carrying out independent economic activity
- Includes legal persons, natural persons, state bodies (when acting as taxable persons)
- Economic activity: any activity of producers, traders, service providers

### s 4 - Registracna povinnost (Registration Obligation)

**Topics:** registration, thresholds, mandatory, voluntary
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions (2025 rules):
- Mandatory registration threshold: EUR 50,000 turnover in calendar year (s 4 ods. 1)
- Alternative threshold: EUR 62,500 from a single supply in current calendar year
- Registration must be filed within 5 working days of exceeding threshold
- Tax office assigns VAT number within 10 working days (retroactively effective)
- From 2025: turnover calculated per calendar year (reset to zero on Jan 1), NOT rolling 12 months

Conditions:
- Turnover = taxable supplies in Slovakia (not exempt without deduction)
- Threshold applies to domestic taxable persons only

Exceptions:
- Persons making only exempt supplies under ss 37-39 (insurance, financial, real estate) are not required to register
- Voluntary registration available below threshold (s 4 ods. 2)

### s 4c - Danova zaruka (Tax Security)

### s 5 - Registracia zahranicnej osoby (Foreign Person Registration)

## PART 3: TAX LIABILITY / TAX POINT (ss 19-21)

### s 19 - Danova povinnost pri dodani (Tax Point for Supply)

**Topics:** tax point, chargeability, supply of goods/services
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** all sections (determines period)

Key provisions:
- General rule: tax point = date of supply (den dodania)
- If payment before supply: tax point = date of payment (for amount received)
- Continuous/repeated supplies: tax point = last day of period or date of payment
- Construction services with acceptance protocol: date of acceptance

Conditions:
- Invoice date is NOT the tax point (common error)
- Tax point determines which VAT period the transaction belongs to

### s 20 - Danova povinnost pri nadobudnuti (Tax Point for IC Acquisition)

**Topics:** tax point for IC acquisition
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** B1

Key provisions:
- Tax point = 15th day of month following month of acquisition
- If invoice issued before that date: tax point = invoice date

## PART 4: TAX BASE AND RATES (ss 22-27)

### s 22 - Zaklad dane pri dodani (Tax Base for Supply)

**Topics:** tax base, consideration, ancillary costs
**Rates:** N/A (base on which rate is applied)

Key provisions:

- Includes: ancillary costs (packing, transport, insurance)
- Excludes: discounts and rebates granted at time of supply

### s 25 - Oprava zakladu dane (Adjustment of Tax Base)

**Topics:** credit notes, debit notes, corrections
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** C1, C2

Key provisions:
- Tax base adjusted when: order cancelled, goods returned, price reduced/increased
- Credit note required for reductions
- Adjustment relates back to original tax period or current period (depending on circumstance)

### s 25a - Oprava zakladu dane pri uplnom alebo ciastocnom nezaplateni (Non-payment Adjustment)

**Topics:** bad debt relief
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:
- Supplier can adjust tax base if customer does not pay within 150 days
- Customer must correspondingly adjust their input deduction

### s 26 - Prepocet cudzej meny a zaokruhlovanie (Currency and Rounding)

**Topics:** foreign currency, rounding
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** all

Key provisions:
- Foreign currency converted using ECB reference rate on day preceding tax point
- Or NBS rate if ECB rate not available
- Rounding: to 2 decimal places (ROUND_HALF_UP per Slovak practice)

### s 27 - Sadzba dane (Tax Rates) -- CRITICAL SECTION

**Topics:** VAT rates, standard, reduced, zero
**Rates:** 23%, 19%, 5%, 0%
**KV sections:** all (rate determines amount)

Key provisions (effective 2025-01-01):

**Standard rate: 23%**
- Applies to all goods and services not listed in Annex 7 or 7a
- Increased from 20% (pre-2025)

**Reduced rate: 19%**
- Applies to goods listed in Annex 7, point 1
- Applies to services listed in Annex 7a, point 1
- New rate from 2025 (replaced former 10% reduced rate)

**Reduced rate: 5%**
- Applies to goods listed in Annex 7, points 2 and 3
- Applies to services listed in Annex 7a, point 2
- New rate from 2025

**Zero rate: 0%**
- Exports outside EU (s 47)
- Intra-community supplies to VAT-registered persons in other EU MS (s 43)
- International transport services (s 46)

Rate history:
| Period | Standard | Reduced 1 | Reduced 2 |
|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Pre-2025 | 20% | 10% | N/A |
| From 2025-01-01 | 23% | 19% | 5% |

## ANNEXES (Prilohy) -- CRITICAL FOR RATE DETERMINATION

### Priloha 7 - Zoznam tovarov (List of Goods)

#### Point 1: Goods at 19% rate

**Food and beverages (broad category):**
- Imported and wild meat varieties
- Marine fish and seafood
- Cream, processed cheeses, eggs
- Vegetables (not in 5% list), fruits, nuts, grains
- Oils and fats
- Coffee, tea, spices, cocoa
- Chocolate, confectionery, sweets
- Bread (not in 5% list), pasta, baked goods
- Prepared and preserved foods (canned goods, instant meals)
- Non-alcoholic beverages (except mineral water in some cases)
- Fruit juices with added sugar
- Salt, vinegar, sauces, condiments

**Other goods at 19%:**
- Medicines and selected medical devices
- Electricity
- Gas and heating

#### Point 2: Goods at 5% rate

**Essential domestic food items:**
- Domestic beef, fresh or chilled (maso z hovadzich zvierat, cerstve alebo chladene)
- Domestic pork, fresh or chilled
- Domestic sheep and goat meat, fresh or chilled
- Domestic poultry meat, fresh or chilled
- Freshwater fish and fillets
- Unprocessed milk (mlieko)
- Yogurt and fermented dairy products
- Butter (maslo)
- Sheep bryndza cheese (bryndza ovcia)
- Fresh vegetables (select domestic): potatoes, carrots, onions, cabbage, tomatoes
- Fresh fruit (select domestic): apples, pears, cherries, plums, strawberries
- Honey
- Fresh bread and basic baked goods (without additives)
- Fruit juices without added sugar (100% juice)

#### Point 3: Additional goods at 5% rate

- Books and printed periodicals (if advertising content < 50%, erotic content < 10%)
- E-books and audiobooks (from 2025)

### Priloha 7a - Zoznam sluzieb (List of Services)

#### Point 1: Services at 19% rate

- Restaurant and catering services - serving of food and non-alcoholic beverages
  - Excludes alcoholic beverages (> 0.5% ABV) -- those at 23%
  - Delivery/takeaway food services (e.g., Wolt, Bolt delivery fee at 23%)

#### Point 2: Services at 5% rate

- Accommodation services (hotels, guesthouses, hostels, camping)
- Sports event admission fees
- Fitness center and gym access fees
- Goods and services supplied by social enterprises (with 100% profit reinvestment)

### Rate determination logic for engine:

```
IF goods/services in Annex 7 point 2/3 OR Annex 7a point 2:
    rate = 5%
ELIF goods/services in Annex 7 point 1 OR Annex 7a point 1:
    rate = 19%
ELIF exempt transaction (ss 28-41):
    rate = EXEMPT (no VAT)
ELIF exempt with deduction (ss 43-48):
    rate = 0%
ELSE:
    rate = 23%
```

## PART 6: INPUT VAT DEDUCTION (ss 49-64)

### s 49 - Odpocitanie dane platitelom (Deduction by VAT Payer)

**Topics:** input VAT deduction right
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** B1, B2, B3

Key provisions:
- VAT payer may deduct input VAT on goods/services used for taxable supplies
- Deduction right arises when tax becomes chargeable (tax point of supplier)
- Must have valid invoice (or customs document for imports)
- Must be for business purpose

Conditions:
- Only registered VAT payers (s 4 or s 5) can deduct
- s 7a registrants: NO deduction right (they only self-assess)

### s 49a - Odpocitanie dane pri nehnutelnosti (Real Estate Deduction)

**Topics:** real estate VAT deduction
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** B2

### s 50 - Pomerne odpocitanie dane (Proportional Deduction / Coefficient)

**Topics:** partial deduction, coefficient, mixed use
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:
- When goods/services used for BOTH taxable and exempt supplies
- Deduction coefficient = taxable supplies / total supplies (excluding certain items)
- Coefficient calculated annually, provisional coefficient used during year
- Year-end adjustment required
- Coefficient rounded up to 2 decimal places

Formula:
```
coefficient = taxable_turnover / (taxable_turnover + exempt_turnover)
deductible_vat = input_vat * coefficient
```

Exceptions to denominator:
- Occasional financial transactions excluded
- Occasional real estate transactions excluded
- Self-supply excluded

### s 51 - Uplatnenie prava na odpocitanie (Application of Deduction)

**Topics:** deduction exercise, time limits
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** B1, B2, B3

Key provisions:
- Deduction exercised in VAT return for period when conditions are met
- If not exercised in that period: can be exercised in any of the following 12 months
- Must have invoice at time of exercising deduction
- From 2025: deduction allowed for IC acquisitions even without invoice if invoice not received by filing deadline

### s 53, s 53a - Uprava odpocitanej dane (Adjustment of Deducted VAT)

**Topics:** adjustment of deduction, change of use
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:
- If purpose of goods/services changes after deduction, adjustment required
- Change from taxable to exempt use: repay proportional VAT
- Change from exempt to taxable use: additional deduction allowed

### s 54, s 54a-d - Uprava odpocitanej dane pri investicnom majetku (Investment Property Adjustment)

**Topics:** capital goods adjustment, 5-year, 20-year
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:
- Movable capital goods (threshold EUR 1,700+): 5-year adjustment period (from 2025)
- Immovable property (buildings): 20-year adjustment period (from 2025)
- Annual adjustment: 1/5 or 1/20 of original deduction
- Triggered by change in proportion of taxable vs exempt use

### ss 55-64 - Vratenie dane (VAT Refunds)

**Topics:** refund to domestic persons, foreign persons, travelers
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** N/A

Key provisions:

- ss 56-58: Refund to foreign persons (reciprocity basis for non-EU)
- ss 59-60: Tax-free shopping (refund to non-EU travelers, threshold EUR 100)

## PART 8: ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS (ss 69-83)

### s 69 - Osoby povinne platit dan (Persons Liable to Pay Tax) -- REVERSE CHARGE

**Topics:** reverse charge, self-assessment, domestic, cross-border
**Rates:** Slovak rate applies (self-assessed by recipient)
**KV sections:** A2 (output, domestic RC), B1 (input, all RC)

Key provisions:

**s 69 ods. 1** - General rule: supplier is liable to pay tax

**s 69 ods. 2** - Cross-border services (B2B): recipient is liable
- Services received from foreign persons where place of supply is Slovakia
- Recipient self-assesses Slovak VAT

**s 69 ods. 3** - IC acquisition: acquirer is liable
- Self-assessment of VAT on goods acquired from other EU MS

**s 69 ods. 6** - Supply by foreign person: domestic recipient is liable
- When foreign person supplies goods/services in Slovakia
- Domestic VAT payer self-assesses

**s 69 ods. 12** - Domestic reverse charge (tuzemske samozdanenie):
- Construction services (CPA codes 41-43) between VAT payers
- Supply of real property where supplier opted to tax (s 38)
- Supply of metal waste and scrap
- Supply of emission quotas
- Supply of agricultural products (selected)
- Supply of integrated circuits, mobile phones, tablets, laptops (above EUR 5,000 per transaction)

Mechanism:
1. Supplier issues invoice WITHOUT VAT, marked "prenesenie danovej povinnosti"
2. Recipient self-assesses VAT at applicable Slovak rate
3. Recipient reports both output VAT (self-assessed) and input VAT (deduction)
4. Net effect for full VAT payer: zero (output and input cancel out)

### s 78a - Kontrolny vykaz (KV DPH - Control Statement) -- CRITICAL

**Topics:** control statement, anti-fraud reporting
**Rates:** N/A
**KV sections:** all (this defines the sections)

Key provisions:
- Filed with each VAT return (same deadline: 25th of following month)
- Contains detailed invoice-level data matching buyer and seller
- Filed exclusively electronically

**KV DPH Section Structure:**

| Section | Content | Source transactions |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| **A.1** | Issued invoices - standard domestic supply | Supplier issues invoice with VAT to domestic VAT payer |
| **A.2** | Issued invoices - domestic reverse charge | Supplier issues invoice without VAT (s 69 ods. 12) |
| **B.1** | Received invoices - self-assessment | Recipient self-assesses VAT (IC acquisition, services from abroad, domestic RC) |
| **B.2** | Received invoices - input VAT deduction | Recipient claims deduction from domestic supplier invoice |
| **B.3** | Other input documents - summarized | Cash register receipts, fuel receipts, small documents without full invoice data |
| **C.1** | Corrections to issued invoices | Credit notes, debit notes correcting A.1/A.2 invoices |
| **C.2** | Corrections to received invoices | Credit notes correcting B.1/B.2 invoices, corrections to simplified invoices |
| **D.1** | ERP (cash register) sales | Sales recorded through electronic cash register |
| **D.2** | Other output without full invoice | Sales to non-taxable persons, travel tickets, fuel pump documents, highway vignettes |

**A.1 required data per invoice:**
- Customer VAT ID (IC DPH)
- Invoice number
- Invoice date
- Tax base per rate
- VAT amount per rate
- Total amount

**B.2 required data per invoice:**
- Supplier VAT ID (IC DPH)
- Invoice number
- Invoice date
- Tax base
- VAT amount
- Deduction proportion
