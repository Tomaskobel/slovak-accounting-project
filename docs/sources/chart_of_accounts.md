### Groups 75–79 — Podsuvahove ucty (Off-balance Sheet Accounts)

Off-balance sheet accounts are in groups 75 through 79. These are not prescribed at the 3-digit synthetic level — the entity defines its own accounts within this range for tracking:

- Leased assets
- Pledged assets
- Contingent liabilities
- Contingent assets
- Assets in custody
- Written-off receivables still being pursued
- Other off-balance sheet items

Common examples used in practice:
- 751 — Prenajaty majetok (Leased assets)
- 752 — Majetok v uschovni (Assets in custody)
- 753 — Pohladavky odpisane (Written-off receivables tracked)
- 754 — Zalozeny majetok (Pledged assets)
- 755 — Podmienene zavazky (Contingent liabilities)
- 756 — Podmienene pohladavky (Contingent assets)

Note: These are illustrative — each entity chooses its own off-balance sheet account structure.

## Classes 8–9 — Vnutroorganizacne uctovnictvo (Internal Management Accounting)

Classes 8 and 9 are reserved for internal (managerial) accounting. The framework chart of accounts does not prescribe specific account codes in these classes. Each entity designs its own cost accounting structure for:

- Cost center accounting
- Activity-based costing
- Internal transfers
- Budgeting and variance analysis
- Production cost tracking

These accounts do not appear in external financial statements.
